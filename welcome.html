WHAT IS DEVSTREAM?

DevStream is a range of programming courses, coding courses, and developer bootcamps, designed to get you into industry as a web or app developer.

WHO IS IT FOR?

Anyone looking for practical coding and programming courses that give you the industry skills and prepare you for entry level developer roles.

LOCATION?

You’ll be learning to code in the vibrant centre of Dublin, close to Dublin’s Silicon Docks.

HOW DO I APPLY?

Follow the steps we have outlined below and get on your way to an exciting new career!

HANDS ON LEARNING

DevStream is not a typical classroom. Think of it more as developer bootcamp. You will be learning by doing and building, not just by sitting back and listening. We set up our programmes to closely resemble a real work environment.

INDUSTRY FOCUSED

Most classes give instruction, then have people practice on their own. At DevStream, we like to create a developer-led environment reflecting the way things work in industry. Practice happens by applying the theory in group projects.

ENGAGED MENTORSHIP

A big part of learning to be a developer is learning how to get unstuck. You don’t have to do it alone. You will have plenty of one-on-one attention from mentors who can help you navigate the hard parts.
